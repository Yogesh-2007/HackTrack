Core Features

Everything You Need

A complete hackathon management system built with modern web technologies and secure QR verification.

📋
Smart Registration
Participants register with name, email, phone, college, team details, and upload their ID proof. All data securely stored in MongoDB.
🛡️
Admin Verification
Admins review all registrations, approve or reject with reasons, bulk-approve pending participants, and manage the entire attendee list.
📱
Secure QR Codes
Unique QR codes are generated upon approval, embedding a signed JWT token with participant ID and expiration timestamp for tamper-proof entry.
🔍
QR Scanner
Volunteers use the scanner page to check in participants. Duplicate entries are blocked. Every scan is logged with timestamp and volunteer info.
⚖️
Judge Panel
Judges score teams on Innovation, Technical Complexity, and Presentation (0-10 each). Scores are averaged across all judges for fairness.
🏆
Live Leaderboard
Real-time leaderboard ranks teams by aggregated scores. Automatically updates as judges submit evaluations throughout the event.
